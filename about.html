Our Mission

To empower Ugandan women entrepreneurs through targeted micro-investments and support, driving economic growth and gender equality in the informal sector.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for women's economic empowerment in Uganda, creating a thriving ecosystem of successful women-owned businesses that contribute significantly to the country's economic development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and shape our approach

Financial Inclusion

We believe access to capital should be based on business fundamentals and character, not collateral. Our innovative financing solutions break down traditional barriers to create opportunities for talented entrepreneurs.

Community-Driven

Our deep community connections and local presence enable us to understand entrepreneurs' needs and validate their potential through social capital and reputation.

Data-Driven Impact

We measure what matters. Our rigorous approach to tracking both financial and social metrics ensures transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement in our impact.
